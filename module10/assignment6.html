Afro Festival Omaha 2021

The Afro Festival was located at Aksarben Village in Omaha, Ne on September 04, 2021 it included African culture, food, dance, music, and so much more. I loved this festival so much because it showed me more of my culture and I loved the music.

My Favorite Songs In The Festival:

  • Chale-Sho Madjozi
  • Hola Hola-Sugarboy
  • Overload-Mavins
  • Alkayida-Kcee
  • Dr SID-Groove
  • Runtown-Walahi
